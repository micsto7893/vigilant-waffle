What is The Vigilant Waffle

The Vigilant Waffle is a highly serious* internet project dedicated to curiosity, experimentation, and keeping a watchful eye on things that may or may not matter. It exists at the crossroads of creativity, technology, and late-night ‚Äúwhat if I tried this?‚Äù ideas.

Despite its breakfast-themed name, The Vigilant Waffle is less about syrup and more about staying alert, learning in public, and occasionally breaking things just to see how they work. Some ideas succeed, some fail gloriously, and all of them are part of the process.

This space is a home for small projects, half-baked concepts, and fully toasted experiments‚Äîbuilt with curiosity, humor, and just enough vigilance to keep things interesting. No waffles were harmed in the making of this site. Probably.

This site exists to document small experiments, lessons learned, and the occasional gloriously broken idea along the way.
