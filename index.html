What is The Vigilant Waffle

The Vigilant Waffle is a highly serious* internet project dedicated to curiosity, experimentation, and maintaining a watchful eye over things that may or may not matter. It exists at the intersection of creativity, technology, and late-night ‚Äúwhat if I tried this?‚Äù decisions that seemed reasonable at the time.

Despite its breakfast-themed name, The Vigilant Waffle is less about syrup and more about staying alert, learning in public, and occasionally breaking things just to understand how they worked in the first place. Some experiments succeed, some fail gloriously, and a few become cautionary tales for future waffles.

This space serves as a living archive for small projects, half-baked concepts, and fully toasted experiments‚Äî all documented with curiosity, humor, and just enough vigilance to pretend this was the plan all along. No waffles were harmed in the making of this site. Probably.

This site exists to document experiments, lessons learned, and the occasional mandatory requirement, approached with equal parts compliance, creativity, and controlled chaos.

*Results may vary.

Responsive Reality Check Complete

This shenanigan began with the dangerous assumption that a layout which looked excellent on a large desktop monitor would naturally behave itself on smaller screens. The site was clean. The navigation was aligned. Confidence was once again misplaced.

üí° What I learned: Responsive design must be tested early and often. A layout that succeeds on desktop can still fail spectacularly on mobile if responsiveness is treated as an afterthought.
